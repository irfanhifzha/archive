Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

To enroll in a course, simply visit the course page and click the "Enroll Now" button. You will be guided through the registration process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfer. You can choose your preferred payment method at checkout.

How can I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password". You will receive a link to reset your password via email.

Can I get a refund for a course?

Yes, we offer a 14-day refund policy. If you're not satisfied with the course, you can request a refund within 14 days of purchase.
